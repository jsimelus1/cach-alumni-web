A Legacy of Excellence

The CACH Alumni Club is more than a social network; it is the heartbeat of our shared history. Since its founding, the Collège Adventiste du Cap-Haïtien has been a beacon of academic rigor and spiritual formation. Our club exists to ensure that the flame lit during our school years continues to burn brightly across the globe, uniting graduates from every generation.

We believe that our collective success is a reflection of the foundation laid at CACH. By bringing together professionals, leaders, and visionaries, we create a ecosystem of mentorship and opportunity. Whether you graduated recently or decades ago, you belong to a family that spans continents and industries, all bound by the same values of service and integrity.

Through our annual gatherings, scholarship initiatives, and campus support projects, we aim to give back to the institution that gave us so much. We invite you to explore our upcoming events, reconnect with lost classmates, and join us in building a future that honors the heritage of CACH.
